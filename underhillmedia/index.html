Common Questions

Answers before
you even ask.

Still have questions? Email info@underhillmedia.com

How far in advance should I book? −
For weddings, 6–12 months is ideal — popular dates fill quickly. Corporate, legal, and real estate shoots can usually be booked within a few weeks. Reach out early to hold your date.
Do you travel outside Huntsville and The Shoals? +
Absolutely. We serve all of Alabama and travel nationally for the right project. Travel fees may apply for destinations more than 60 miles from Huntsville. Just ask — we'll make it work.
What is legal videography and do I need it? +
Legal videography covers video depositions, day-in-the-life documentaries, settlement documentaries, and scene documentation. If you're an Alabama attorney handling a personal injury or disability case, video evidence can be enormously powerful. We deliver court-admissible files.
Do you offer drone footage? +
Yes. Aerial drone footage is available as an add-on for real estate, weddings, events, and corporate projects. All drone operations are FAA Part 107 compliant.
How long until I receive my finished video? +
Real estate videos: 24–48 hours. Corporate and event videos: 5–10 business days. Wedding films: 4–8 weeks depending on length and complexity. Rush delivery available.
Do I own my footage? +
Yes. Once payment is complete, you receive full usage rights to your delivered video. We retain the right to use footage in our portfolio unless you request otherwise in writing.
Can you match our brand style? +
Yes. We review your brand guidelines and existing content before production. Color grading, music, and delivery formats are all matched to your brand identity.
