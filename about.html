Slapstick Hockey began as a way to share the funniest, weirdest, and most human moments in the game — the stuff that never makes the highlight reel anywhere else. What started as a passion project has grown into a content brand built by people who genuinely love hockey and love making people laugh.

Our Story

From the stands to your feed

Slapstick Hockey started as a hobby — a way to capture the chaos, comedy, and chemistry that happens at the rink and share it with anyone who'd watch. No big studio, no big budget, just a camera, a mic, and a love for the game.

Over time, that hobby turned into a real content brand. We've covered local tournaments, charity games, and league nights, built partnerships with hockey brands and organizations, and grown a community of fans who show up for the laughs as much as the highlights.

We're still doing what we started doing — just with better gear and a bigger crew.
